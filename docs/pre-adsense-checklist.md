# Pre-AdSense launch checklist  
_All The Calculators_

This document tracks everything required to prepare **All The Calculators** for Google AdSense approval.

The goal is to ensure the site:
- meets AdSense policy requirements
- looks complete and trustworthy
- feels like a real, finished product

---

## 1. Required site pages

These pages must exist and be reachable from the footer.

### ⬜ About page
**Status:** Not started / In progress / Complete

Must include:
- What All The Calculators is
- Who it’s for
- What problems it solves
- Clear statement that calculators provide estimates only

Guidelines:
- Plain English
- Calm, neutral tone
- No marketing fluff
- No “AI-powered” claims unless essential

---

### ⬜ Contact page
**Status:** Not started / In progress / Complete

Must include at least one:
- Contact email (preferably domain-based)
- OR contact form

Notes:
- AdSense frequently rejects sites without clear contact information

---

### ⬜ Privacy policy (mandatory)
**Status:** Not started / In progress / Complete

Must explicitly mention:
- Google AdSense
- Cookies
- Third-party vendors
- How users can opt out of personalised ads

Recommended:
- Use a generator (Termly, iubenda, FreePrivacyPolicy)
- Lightly edit for clarity and accuracy

---

### ⬜ Cookie notice (recommended)
**Status:** Not started / In progress / Complete

UK-friendly, simple banner:
- “We use cookies for analytics and advertising”
- Link to Privacy Policy

---

## 2. Sitemap & discoverability

### ⬜ Sitemap.xml
**Status:** Not started / In progress / Complete

Requirements:
- Accessible at `/sitemap.xml`
- Includes:
  - Homepage
  - Category pages
  - All calculator pages
  - About page
  - Contact page
  - Privacy policy

---

## 3. Footer completeness

### ⬜ Footer links
**Status:** Not started / In progress / Complete

Footer must include:
- About
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Sitemap

Optional but recommended:
- “Estimates only” note
- © current year

---

## 4. Calculator quality checks

All calculators must meet the locked Calculator Page Template.

### ⬜ General checks (apply to every calculator)
- Page loads without errors
- Calculator works without JS errors
- Inputs start blank
- Results show placeholders before calculation
- Reset works correctly
- Mobile layout works cleanly
- Keyboard navigation works
- Errors are accessible (`aria-describedby`)

---

### ⬜ Content checks
- Clear purpose stated near the top
- Results visible without scrolling too far
- No “coming soon” calculator pages
- No empty or thin pages

---

## 5. Health & salary calculator safety

### ⬜ Health calculators
Confirm:
- No diagnoses
- No goals or judgemental language
- Clear disclaimer present
- Neutral framing only

---

### ⬜ Salary calculators
Confirm:
- “Estimates only” language
- No guarantees
- No tax advice framing
- No claims of accuracy vs HMRC

---

## 6. Ads readiness (pre-approval)

Before AdSense approval:
- ⬜ No ad placeholders
- ⬜ No “ads go here” boxes
- ⬜ No fake ads
- ⬜ No affiliate links disguised as calculators

After approval:
- Start with Auto ads
- Refine placement later

---

## 7. Analytics (recommended)

### ⬜ Analytics installed
**Status:** Optional / Complete

Options:
- Google Analytics
- Plausible
- Simple pageview tracking

Notes:
- Not required for approval
- Helpful for RPM optimisation later

---

## 8. Domain & technical checks

### ⬜ Domain & hosting
- Custom domain active
- HTTPS enabled
- No default Netlify pages visible

---

### ⬜ SEO basics
- Unique `<title>` for every page
- Unique meta descriptions
- No duplicate titles like “Calculator — All The Calculators”

---

## 9. Final pre-submission checklist

Before applying for AdSense, confirm:

- ⬜ About page live
- ⬜ Contact page live
- ⬜ Privacy policy live
- ⬜ Footer links complete
- ⬜ Sitemap accessible
- ⬜ All calculators functional
- ⬜ Mobile experience solid
- ⬜ Site feels “finished”

If the answer to this is “yes”, you’re ready:

> “Would I trust this site if I found it via Google?”

---

## 10. Submission notes

When applying for AdSense:
- Apply using the **root domain**
- Choose “Content site”
- Do not rush changes after submission
- Review usually takes a few days to a couple of weeks

---

## Definition of done

This checklist is complete when:
- All required pages are live
- No blockers remain
- AdSense application is submitted
